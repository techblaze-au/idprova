## Email Body

Dear NCCoE AI Identity Team,

Please find attached Tech Blaze Consulting's feedback on the NCCoE concept paper "Accelerating the Adoption of Software and AI Agent Identity and Authorization" (published February 5, 2026).

**Who we are:** Tech Blaze Consulting Pty Ltd is an Australian cybersecurity consultancy based in Canberra, ACT, specialising in security assessment, compliance, and enterprise architecture.

**What we're submitting:** A detailed response presenting IDProva, an open protocol (Apache 2.0) for verifiable AI agent identity. IDProva uses W3C DIDs, scoped delegation tokens, and hash-chained audit trails to address agent identification, authorization, auditing, and credential lifecycle management.

**Prior engagement:** Tech Blaze Consulting filed a response to the NIST Collaborative AI Security Initiative Request for Information (NIST-2025-0035).

**Coverage summary:** IDProva addresses 5 of 6 NCCoE focus areas at full coverage, with partial coverage on prompt injection mitigation (active development underway). The attached document provides detailed protocol-level responses to each focus area.

**Engagement interest:**
